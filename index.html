About LIC Cancer Covers

LIC Cancer Covers is a cancer insurance policy offered by Life Insurance Corporation of India. It is designed to provide financial protection to individuals and their families in case they are diagnosed with cancer.

Coverage

  • Sum assured ranging from Rs.10,000 to Rs.50 lakhs
  • Coverage for all stages of cancer
  • Cashless hospitalization facility
  • Income tax benefits
  • Flexible premium payment options

Benefits

  • Lump sum payout on diagnosis of cancer
  • No medical examination required
  • Policy can be bought online
  • Policy can be renewed for up to 20 years
  • Policy can be surrendered after completion of 3 years

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Cancer Covers?

The minimum age to buy LIC Cancer Covers is 18 years.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Cancer Covers?

The maximum age to buy LIC Cancer Covers is 65 years.

Can I buy multiple policies of LIC Cancer Covers?

Yes, you can buy multiple policies of LIC Cancer Covers as long as the total sum assured does not exceed Rs.50 lakhs.
